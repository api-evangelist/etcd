Jepsen tested and analyzed etcd 3.4.3, and had both good results and useful feedback to share with us. A key part of etcd’s design is strong consistency guarantees across the distributed key-value store. Kubernetes, Rook, OpenStack, and countless other critical software projects rely on etcd, in part, because of the etcd project’s focus on reliability and correctness.
